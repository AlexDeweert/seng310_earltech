Hierarchical task analysis

  1. Obtain UVIC login credentials and create profile
  2. Open the CalendarCompanion application
    1. Click on virtual assistant chat
      1. Engage in dialogue with virtual assistant
    2. Click on “view all courses for current semester”.
    3. Click on “study tips, tricks, habits, and helpful apps.”
    4. Click on “generate suggested schedule”.
      1. Click on “attempt registration”
        1. Success
        2. (failed) Click on “re-generate suggested schedule” (go back to 5.e.i)
      2. Click “Remove a course from potential registration pool”
    5. Click on “Your schedule and campus map”
    6. Click on “Campus mapping”
    7. Click on “Co-op information”
    8. Click on “Print course-outline”
      1. Click PDF option
      2. Click paper option
    9. Click on “Semester preference”
    10. Click on “Exchange information”
    11. Click on “Online Learning information”
    12. Link CalendarCompanion to UVIC Account
